Memelas

By: Yatxil Arroyo-Rocha

Memelas are known as various names across differnt states in Mexico. Either way, memelas are at the simplest: a seasoned corn tortilla with beans and cheese. The recipe will differ across states.

Ingredients

  • Masa de maíz
  • Beans
  • Cheese
  • Aciento

Instructions

  1. Flatten out the masa into a circle and pintch the edges all around
  2. Set it on a pan to cook
  3. While cooking, prepare your beans and cheese to your prefrence
  4. Once done cooking, add your aciento, beans, and cheese
